## Description
*Maximum 4000 characters*

**STOPWATCH WITH SIMPLE LAP TRACKING**

Averaging Stopwatch is a powerful yet simple timing tool designed for athletes, coaches, and anyone who needs precise lap timing with instant performance analytics.

**KEY FEATURES**

• Real-time lap tracking with millisecond precision
• Instant average calculations for completed and active laps
• Automatic fastest/slowest lap highlighting
• Current lap timer with total elapsed time display
• Clean, easy-to-read interface optimized for quick glances
• Export all lap data to CSV for detailed analysis
• Works completely offline - no internet required
• No ads, no tracking, no subscriptions

**PERFECT FOR:**

Runners tracking interval training splits
Swimmers monitoring lap consistency
Cyclists measuring circuit times
Coaches timing multiple athletes
Students conducting timed experiments
Anyone needing accurate performance metrics

**HOW IT WORKS**

Start the timer and tap the lap button at each interval. The app instantly calculates your average lap time and highlights your best and worst performances in real-time. The large, clear display shows your current lap time, making it easy to monitor your pace during activity.

**EXPORT YOUR DATA**

All lap times can be exported to CSV format with timestamps, individual lap durations, cumulative times, and statistics. Perfect for tracking progress over time or sharing data with coaches.

## App Review Notes
*Information for the Apple review team*

This is a simple stopwatch application with lap timing functionality.

**How to test:**
1. Tap the green Play button to start the timer
2. Tap the orange Flag button to record lap times
3. Tap the blue Check button to complete the final lap and stop
4. Tap the gray Reset button to clear all data
5. Tap "Export Data" to download lap times as CSV

The app displays:
- Current lap time (large display)
- Total elapsed time (below current lap)
- Average times for completed laps and overall average
- Fastest lap highlighted in green, slowest in red

All functionality is available immediately without any setup or account creation.
